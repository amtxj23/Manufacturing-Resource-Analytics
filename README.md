# 📖 Project Overview

Modern manufacturing industries generate thousands of production records every day. Without proper analytics, organizations struggle to identify the root causes of production defects, rising operational costs, equipment downtime, and declining workforce productivity.

This project simulates the work of a Manufacturing Data Analyst by designing a relational SQL database, transforming raw manufacturing data into a structured data warehouse, and performing advanced business analytics to generate actionable insights for executive decision-making.

The complete project demonstrates how raw operational data can be converted into strategic intelligence using SQL and Business Intelligence techniques.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

A manufacturing company has observed:

- Increasing defect rates
- Rising production costs
- High machine downtime
- Supplier quality inconsistencies
- Declining workforce productivity
- Inefficient energy consumption

The management team requires a centralized analytics system capable of identifying operational bottlenecks, measuring production performance, and supporting data-driven decision making.

# Business Questions Answered

### Production Analytics
- Which production batches incur the highest operational cost?
- What factors drive production efficiency?

### Quality Analytics
- Which quality metrics contribute most to manufacturing defects?
- How does supplier quality influence defect rates?

### Workforce Analytics
- Does worker productivity reduce production defects?
- What is the relationship between safety incidents and operational downtime?

### Maintenance Analytics
- How does maintenance affect machine downtime?
- Is preventive maintenance reducing production losses?

### Operational Analytics
- Which processes consume the highest energy?
- Which operational factors contribute most to production cost?
